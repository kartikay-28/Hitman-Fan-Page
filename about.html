About Rohit Sharma

Who is Rohit Sharma?

Rohit Gurunath Sharma (born 30 April 1987) is an Indian cricketer and the captain of the Indian national team in ODIs and Tests. Known as The Hitman, he is regarded as one of the greatest opening batsmen in cricket history.

Early Life & Domestic Career

Rohit Sharma was born in Bansod, Nagpur, Maharashtra. He was raised by his grandparents and uncles due to financial struggles. His cricket journey began at a young age, and he trained under Dinesh Lad, who recognized his talent and encouraged him to switch from off-spin bowling to batting. He made his debut for Mumbai in domestic cricket, where he impressed everyone with his batting skills.

International Career

  • Made his ODI debut in 2007 against Ireland.
  • Scored his first international century in 2010.
  • Promoted as an opener in 2013, which changed his career forever.
  • Scored a world-record 264 runs in an ODI against Sri Lanka in 2014.
  • Only batsman in history to score three double centuries in ODIs.
  • Led India to victory in the 2024 ICC T20 World Cup as captain.

Playing Style & Records

Rohit Sharma is known for his effortless strokeplay, powerful sixes, and the ability to accelerate innings. He holds the record for the most sixes in international cricket, surpassing Chris Gayle.

Captaincy & Leadership

Rohit took over the captaincy of Mumbai Indians in 2013 and led them to five IPL titles, making him one of the most successful IPL captains. In 2023, he became the full-time captain of the Indian cricket team, leading them to major victories, including the 2024 ICC T20 World Cup.

Personal Life

Rohit Sharma is married to Ritika Sajdeh, and they have a daughter named Samaira. He is also an animal lover and an ambassador for WWF India, working towards wildlife conservation.

Awards & Achievements

  • Arjuna Award (2015)
  • Khel Ratna Award (2020)
  • Most sixes in international cricket history
  • Highest individual score in ODIs (264)
