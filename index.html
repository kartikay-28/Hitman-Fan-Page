About Rohit Sharma

Rohit Gurunath Sharma (born 30 April 1987) is an Indian cricketer and captain of the Indian national team.

Rohit holds numerous cricket records, including the highest individual score (264) in an ODI.

Achievements

  • Most runs in T20 Internationals
  • Most sixes in international cricket
  • Only player with 3 double centuries in ODIs
  • Winner of 2024 ICC T20 World Cup
  • 5-time IPL Champion (Mumbai Indians)
